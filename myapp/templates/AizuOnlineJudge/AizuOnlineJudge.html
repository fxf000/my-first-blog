Aizu Online Judge

I who haven't studied information field solved Aizu Online Judege problems with python3 as part of learning the basic of programming. I chose the Aize Online Judege because the basic steps to learn programming were easiest to understand. However, the Aize Online Judege problems have been solved with such as C++, and python is said to be slower than C++, so there is a possibility that time constraints of run of the program cannot be kept.

Due to the influence of copyrights such as Aizu Online Judge's commentary books, the content to be described is mainly written in code. Then, if you have any technical advice and so on, please feel free to contact me.
